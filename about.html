About TradingMonkey AI Ltd

Our mission is clear: to develop a reliable, fully-automated, AI-enhanced trading system designed to outperform traditional day-trading strategies. Every aspect of our system is backed by transparent data, continuous performance monitoring, and real-world profitability validation.
